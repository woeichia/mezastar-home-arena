# Changelog — Automatic first-attack flow and charge

## Added

- Attack-order reveal automatically opens the first attacker's Battle Wheel.
- A successful wheel result automatically begins Attack Charge.
- Miss automatically skips charge and advances the test round.
- Charge runs for 2.5 seconds and caps at 12 taps.
- Charge tiers: ×0.90, ×1.00, ×1.10 and ×1.20.
- Maximum charge automatically opens the Rainbow timing stage.
- Rainbow centre hit locks ×1.30; otherwise maximum charge remains ×1.20.
- Wheel and charge multipliers are stored separately for later damage calculation.
- Locked result automatically advances the temporary round flow.
- Player 1's Battle Wheel, charge pad and Rainbow timing interface rotate 180° for the top-side player.
- Player 2's attack interface remains in the standard bottom-side orientation.

## Normal player inputs

- Confirm attacker
- Tap Race
- Stop Battle Wheel
- Tap to charge
- Tap Rainbow timing

No normal navigation button is required between these stages.
